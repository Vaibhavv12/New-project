INFORMATION ABOUT HOSTEL-

Walchand College Of Engineering has a in campus hostel .It spread in about 1.5 acres .It has 2 seperate hostels for boys and girls .Boys Hostel has 4 hostel blocks namely D-5 ,D-6 ,D-7 ,D-8 ; providing access for First year , Second year ,Third year and diploma students. Boys hostel provides with a built in playground , a mess , a store room , a tv hall and boiler area (are where hot water is provided) ; moreover each floor has a seperate heater for hot water .Each room has two cupboard ,3 set of hangers ,1 tube light ,1 Fan ,4 charging sockets etc . Presently it is a 3 shairing room . Centre of the hostel has a maintained garden . Girls hostel also has 4 blocks namely D-1 ,D-2 ,D-3 ,D-4 ; providing access for First year , Second year ,Third year ,Final year and diploma student . Similar facilities are also provided to girls hostel . Students comming from other states accomodate cyber hostel .
